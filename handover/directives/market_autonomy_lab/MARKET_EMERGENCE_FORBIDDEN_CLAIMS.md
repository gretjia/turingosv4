# Market Emergence Forbidden Claims

This packet may use only candidate language.

Allowed only after final audit `PROCEED`:

```text
market emergence candidate pending final audit
```

Forbidden without separate user/architect ratification:

```text
market emergence proven
market mechanism shipped
E2 achieved
E3 achieved
E4 achieved
production-ready market mechanism
ship evidence
```

Candidate sublabels allowed:

```text
E2 replicated candidate
Two-sided market candidate
E3 candidate pending audit
E4 candidate pending audit
```

Any report that uses stronger wording must be downgraded or explicitly
ratified under the project claim taxonomy.
